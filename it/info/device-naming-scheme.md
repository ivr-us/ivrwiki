# Device naming scheme

Devices in out department are given the following way:

`IVR-[1-4]-[PC/NB/TB][XX]`

:::tip
If you are unsure of the last number, ask the Domain Administrator (Susanne Becker) to tell you
:::

## Examples
- The 5th notebook in Macro is called IVR-1-NB5
- The 10th tabled in micro is called IVR-2-TB10
